Continuous Ambulatory Peritoneal Dialysis (CAPD)

Description

Dianeal Ambuflex is indicated for patients with chronic renal failure...

Uses:

  • Dianeal Ambuflex Indications:
    Dianeal Ambuflex is indicated for patients with chronic renal failure who are being maintained on peritoneal dialysis. Peritoneal dialysis is recommended for patients with acute or chronic renal failure when nondialytic medical treatments are deemed insufficient.
  • Dianeal UltraBag Indications:
    Dianeal peritoneal dialysis solutions in UltraBag containers are indicated for use in patients with chronic renal failure undergoing continuous ambulatory peritoneal dialysis when nondialytic medical therapy is considered inadequate.

Advantages:

  • Dianeal, the first commercially available solution for Continuous Ambulatory Peritoneal Dialysis (CAPD), has been providing dependable outcomes for dialysis patients, leveraging 30 years of trusted experience.
