About TBC Healthcare

The story of our evolution

1996

Established as Authorised Channel partner for M/s. Johnson & Johnson - Medical, we were able to expand our services to Major Institutions and Leading Nursing Homes and Dialysis Centers.

2001

Started handling the CAPD Products of M/s. Baxter (India) Pvt. Ltd, servicing about 150 Dialysis Patients and all Major Renal Centers in Chennai.

2010

Our company advertisement was showcased in Cardio Vascular surgeon conference's magazine published by Government Hospital, Chennai.

2013

Marketed a new landmark to our products range to start marketing & sales of Fresenius Medical care - Dialysis Machines.

2016

Expanded South Tamilnadu, India operation to our Madurai warehouse with dedicated Sales Team & Admin staff.
